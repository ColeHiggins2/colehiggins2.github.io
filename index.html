The best things in life are those that require hard work and persistence. Taking those small steps, in the beginning, can be the hardest, but never giving up and not letting anyone bring you down is the only way to achieve greatness. Second, is having a great attitude. A close mentor of mine once said, "The most important thing in life and work is having a positive ATTITUDE! having a good attitude overcomes any shortcomings and can help anyone grow".
